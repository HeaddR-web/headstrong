Privacy Policy — HeaddR Wallpapers

Last updated: 12 June 2026

HeaddR Wallpapers ("we", "the app") is a small tool that publishes our own wallpaper pins to our own Pinterest account through the official Pinterest API. This policy explains what data the app handles.

What the app does

The app reads pre-prepared image and text content and creates pins on a Pinterest board that we own. It is used for our own marketing only and does not act on behalf of, or collect data from, any other Pinterest users.

Data we access and store

  • A Pinterest access token for our own account, obtained via Pinterest's standard OAuth consent. It is stored locally on our own computer and is used solely to publish our pins.
  • Our own board identifier, so pins land on the correct board.

We do not collect, sell, or share personal data of any third parties. We do not run analytics on visitors.

Third-party services

The app communicates with Pinterest. Your use of Pinterest is governed by Pinterest's own Privacy Policy. Products are sold via Gumroad, governed by Gumroad's privacy policy.

Data retention & revocation

The access token can be revoked at any time from the Pinterest account settings, which immediately stops the app's access. Locally stored credentials can be deleted at any time.
